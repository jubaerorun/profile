About MeGIS and Remote Sensing Expert

Hi, my name is Al Jubaer and I am a Project Officer at Jahangirnagar University's Institute of Remote Sensing and GIS under the ICIMOD Project. The Department of Geography and Environment at Jahangirnagar University is where I completed both my Bachelor of Science and Master of Science degrees, and the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the Royal University of Dhaka is where I completed my second Master of Science degree.

I have extensive experience with all of the following software packages: Arc Map, Arc GIS Pro, ArGIS Online, QGIS, Erdas Imagine, Google Earth Engine, ENVI, Survey123, CoBo Toolbox, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Indesign, Adobe XD, and Adobe InCopy, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effect, and Cinema 4D, Adobe Dimension, Google Earth, and Sketchup, AutoCAD, Civil 3D, 3DS Max, Jupyter Notebook (Python, Deep Learning & Machine Learning), Google Colab, Web GIS (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Lefleat GIS, OpenLayer), and Microsoft Excel..
